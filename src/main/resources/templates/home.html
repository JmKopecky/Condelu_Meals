Always Healthy

All of our food is delivered in healthy, well-balanced portions. In fact, our entire menu is vegetarian; no meats, processed or otherwise, to be found. We know the stigma of vegetarian food tasting poorly, but don't be concerned; each bite has been designed specifically to maximize both taste and nutrition.

Ethics Never Cast Aside

All of this might sound too good to be true - surely we must have made compromises somewhere. But no! Through collaborations with our community to source produce, as well as fostering skilled employees with reasonable pays, we have successfully built a team that can produce fresh meals for customers in an ethical manner. We even work with environmental organizations to capture all carbon released to the atmosphere - in equal measures!

Yet Still Delivered Fast

This focus on quality healthy food hasn't come at the cost of convenience, however. We understand you have busy lives, with no way to cook on the road, or no energy to prepare your own meal at home. That's why we've modeled ourselves off fast food restaurants, yet with no compromise to quality.

Here's Our Story

We started small, with one minor location in Houston. But through collaboration with our community, we were able to succeed. We were able to develop a vision for the industry. You see, fast food is renowned for being unhealthy at best and lethal at worst. But we don't believe that it has to be this way.

So we set out to develop a fast food chain with a central, guiding goal: to bridge the gap between convenience and health. Our idea was well received, and we were granted the opportunity to expand into more communities, yet we never lost these core principles.

We Can Help Each Other

As an important part of building a community with every location, we source our food from local farmers. But to get that food to our restaurants on time, we need a team to transport the goods — not to mention staffing each location for our customers.

That's where you come in! We hire locally, providing a multitude of flexible jobs with reasonable pay and hours to our communities. Students and adults alike are encouraged to apply and develop entry-level job experience — there's plenty of work to go around.
